MBA Jargon
Marketing
The 4 Ps
The four levers every marketer controls: Product, Price, Place, Promotion.
P
Product
what you make
P
Price
what you charge
P
Place
where you sell
P
Promotion
how you tell
Coined by E. Jerome McCarthy in 1960, popularised by Philip Kotler. Change one P and the other three have to answer for it.
