## Caption

A value proposition is not a slogan. It is a claim you could be proven wrong about.

Strong ones are testable, we deliver in ten minutes can be checked against the clock. Weak ones hide behind best-in-class service, which means nothing and risks nothing. It answers the only question the listener is really asking: why should I care?

If your promise cannot be falsified, it cannot be believed either.

Defensible in 15 minutes.
