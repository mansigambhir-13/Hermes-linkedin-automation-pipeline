## Caption

Every pitch lives or dies on one sentence: the ask. The exact, quantified thing you want.

A strong ask says what you need, what you give back, and where the money goes. A weak one is vague or buried, and attention does not wait. DocSend found investors spend under four minutes on a deck on average.

If they remember one line, make sure it is the one where you asked.

Defensible in 15 minutes.
