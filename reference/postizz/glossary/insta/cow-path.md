## Caption

Cows wander a hillside and wear a crooked path. Centuries later we pave it, and the crookedness becomes permanent.

Michael Hammer used the image for software. Automate a broken process and you do not fix it, you make it faster and harder to ever change. The detour is now set in concrete.

Before automating the work, ask why the work is shaped that way at all.
