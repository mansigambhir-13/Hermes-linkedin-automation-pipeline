## Caption

Most companies buy their parts. SpaceX builds about eighty-five percent of a rocket itself.

Vertical integration means making in-house what others outsource. It becomes a weapon when the supply chain's prices are set by convention rather than physics, when the markup is tradition, not cost.

Sometimes the cheapest supplier is the one you build yourself.

Read it. Now defend it.
