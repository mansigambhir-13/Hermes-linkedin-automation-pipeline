## Caption

In every system there is a spot where a small push moves everything. Find it and you barely have to shove.

Donella Meadows mapped twelve such leverage points, from tweaking numbers at the weak end to shifting the whole paradigm at the strong one. Most people push hardest on the levers that move least.

The art is not effort. It is knowing where to apply it.
