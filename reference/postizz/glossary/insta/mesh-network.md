## Caption

Old logistics funnels everything through a few giant hubs. A mesh lets every facility route to every other one.

Delhivery runs this way: when a bottleneck appears, parcels reroute in real time instead of waiting on one central sort. No single hub is a single point of failure.

The strongest networks have no center to break.
