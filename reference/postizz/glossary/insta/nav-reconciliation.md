## Caption

A fund's net asset value is the number everyone trusts. Reconciliation is what earns that trust.

NAV reconciliation checks the fund's own valuation against the custodian's, line by line. If positions, cash and profit all agree, the NAV agrees too, it is the last gate before the number goes out the door.

The figure investors rely on is only as honest as the check behind it.
