## Caption (copy-paste)

*Dark Store*

There is a shop near you that you can never walk into. No aisles, no customers, no signage. Only orders.

A dark store is a small fulfilment unit, a couple of thousand square feet, parked inside the neighbourhood it serves. *Blinkit*, *Zepto* and *Swiggy Instamart* run on them. The whole format exists to win minutes, not metres.

Ten-minute delivery is not a logistics trick. It is a real-estate decision.
