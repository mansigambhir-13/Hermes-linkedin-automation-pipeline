## Caption (copy-paste)

*Numeric Distribution*

There are two ways to count how widely a product sells, and they tell different stories.

Numeric distribution is pure reach: the share of all stores that stock you. *Parle* leads here, sitting in more shops than *Britannia*. It says nothing about whether those shops are the ones that matter.

Being in the most stores and being in the right stores are not the same win.
