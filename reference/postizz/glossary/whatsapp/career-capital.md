## Caption (copy-paste)

*Career Capital*

Passion does not come first. Rare and valuable skills do, and the passion tends to follow them.

*Cal Newport* called it *career capital* — the stock of hard-won skills that compounds. Each new capability makes the others worth more. Titles sit still. Capital builds on itself.

Don't follow your passion. Build something so good it can't be ignored.
