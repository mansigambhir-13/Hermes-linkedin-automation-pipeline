## Caption (copy-paste)

*War Premium*

When conflict threatens supply, the price of oil carries a fee that has nothing to do with how much is pumped. It is the market pricing in fear.

After the *Russia-Ukraine* shock, analysts put that war premium near ten to fifteen dollars a barrel. For India, every ten-dollar rise in crude can add roughly half a percent to inflation.

The barrel did not change. The story wrapped around it moved the price.
