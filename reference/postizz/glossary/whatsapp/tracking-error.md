## Caption (copy-paste)

*Tracking Error*

A fund that perfectly copies its index has a tracking error of zero, and adds nothing you could not buy for free.

*Tracking error* measures how far a portfolio strays from its benchmark. Passive funds keep it under two or three percent; active funds run higher because they are placing bets. Too low and you are a closet index; too high and you are off your mandate.

The number quietly reveals how much independent thinking is actually happening.
