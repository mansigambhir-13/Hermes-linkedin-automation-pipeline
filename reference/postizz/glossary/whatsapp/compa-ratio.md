## Caption (copy-paste)

*Compa-Ratio*

There is a number that decides your next raise, and most people never learn it exists.

*Compa-ratio* is your salary divided by the midpoint of your pay band. At 0.80 you sit twenty percent below midpoint, with room to climb. At 1.20 you near the ceiling, and HR quietly starts tapering your raises.

You cannot negotiate against a number you have never been shown.

Read it. Now defend it.
