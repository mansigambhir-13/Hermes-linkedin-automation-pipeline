*Forward Deployed Engineer*

Most software is sold, installed, and left alone. *Palantir* sends an engineer to live inside the customer instead.

A forward deployed engineer sits at the client's site and builds bespoke solutions to their exact problems, until the platform becomes part of how the institution runs. That is not implementation. It is entanglement.

The deepest moat is not a better feature. It is being woven into someone's daily operations.
