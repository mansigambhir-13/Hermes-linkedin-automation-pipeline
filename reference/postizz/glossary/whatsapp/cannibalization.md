## Caption (copy-paste)

*Cannibalization*

A company launches a cheaper product and sales rise. The trap is where the sales came from.

*Cannibalization* is when the new product eats the old one instead of the competition, the customer who would have bought premium reaching for budget instead. Revenue can grow while the brand quietly erodes, for years, before anyone notices.

Growth that feeds on your own margins is not growth. It is a countdown.
