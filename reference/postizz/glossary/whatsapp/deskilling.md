## Caption (copy-paste)

*Deskilling*

Stop doing a thing and the ability to do it fades. Not forgotten exactly, *deskilled*.

When automation takes over a practised task, motor timing slips, mental models drift, diagnosis slows. The operator looks fine until the moment the machine needs them, and then the rust shows.

A skill you no longer use is a skill you are quietly losing.

Read it once. Practice it weekly.
