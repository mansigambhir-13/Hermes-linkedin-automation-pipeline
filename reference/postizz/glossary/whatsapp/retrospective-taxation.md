## Caption (copy-paste)

*Retrospective Taxation*

Picture a deal that was legal the day you signed it, taxed years later under a law written after the fact.

India did exactly that in *2012*, amending the law to reach back to 1962 and tax the *Vodafone*-*Hutchison* transaction. After losing in international arbitration, the government repealed the provision in 2021. Certainty, it turned out, was worth more than the tax.

A rule that rewrites the past collects its real price in lost trust.
