*Perceptual Map*

Plot every brand in a category on two axes of how customers see them, and a picture appears: clusters where everyone crowds, and empty space where no one stands.

That empty space is the prize. A perceptual map is how you find it, *Dove* spotted an unclaimed quadrant once worth around four billion dollars.

The best position on the map is often the one nobody else wanted.
