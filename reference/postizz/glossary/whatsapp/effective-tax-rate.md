## Caption (copy-paste)

*Effective Tax Rate*

The headline tax rate is what the law asks. The effective rate is what a company actually pays after every deduction, credit and clever structure.

The gap can be vast. *Apple*'s Irish operations once paid an effective rate of around 0.005 percent against a statutory 12.5.

The rate on paper is a starting bid. The effective rate is the real story.

Read it. Now defend it.
