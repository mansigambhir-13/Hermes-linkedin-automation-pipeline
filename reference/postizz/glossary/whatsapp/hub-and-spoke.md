*Hub-and-Spoke*

Send everything to one big sorting center, then fan it back out. That is hub-and-spoke, and it is wonderfully efficient until it isn't.

*Blue Dart*'s aviation network runs this way, central hubs connecting regions through one sort. Predictable, high-volume routes love it. Sudden shifts in demand expose it, because the hub is also the single point of failure.

Efficiency and fragility often share the same address.

Read it. Now defend it.
