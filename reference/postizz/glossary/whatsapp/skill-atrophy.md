## Caption (copy-paste)

*Skill Atrophy*

When the machine does the hard part, the human slowly forgets how. That erosion has a name: *skill atrophy*.

Systems that boost output while quietly hollowing out ability are a time bomb. The numbers look great right up until a human has to step in and cannot.

Productivity you cannot sustain without the tool is borrowed, not earned.
