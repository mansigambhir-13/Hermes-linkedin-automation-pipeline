## Caption (copy-paste)

*Identity Lock-in*

The job title you wear starts to feel like who you are. Then any move that means starting over feels like losing yourself.

*Identity lock-in* is that quiet trap. It pushes people to climb within one function, to optimise the title, instead of building rarer skills across several. The résumé looks consistent and the options keep shrinking.

Define yourself by what you can do, not by what you are called.
