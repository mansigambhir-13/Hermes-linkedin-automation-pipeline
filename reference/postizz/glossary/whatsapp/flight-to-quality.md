*Flight to Quality*

When the world feels dangerous, money does not disappear. It moves.

Capital floods out of risky assets and into safe ones — *US Treasuries*, *gold*, the largest stable names. For Indian investors there is a sting in the tail: foreign funds sell Indian equities and buy dollars, so the *Nifty* and the *rupee* tend to fall together.

In a panic, safety is the one asset everybody tries to buy at once.
