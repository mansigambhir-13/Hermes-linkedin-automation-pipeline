Tell managers that exactly ten percent of any team must be rated poor, and you will always find your ten percent, even on a team of stars.

Forced distribution bends every group onto a bell curve whether the people fit it or not. It manufactures a scarcity of top ratings and guarantees that some strong performers get labelled weak.

A quota does not measure talent. It just decides in advance how many will be told they failed.
