Create conceptual illustrations, not photos, on a matte black canvas with generous negative space, emphasizing clarity and symmetrical compositions. Use a primary palette of purples, blues, reds, and greens, applying uneven, subtle-to-bold pastel gradients horizontally to UI elements, titles, and sometimes from top to bottom. Typography should be clean, minimalistic sans-serifs with softened edges, ranging from small, legible text to substantial headings, with some in white and others incorporating gradients. The overall mood should be productive and professional, yet light-hearted and energetic.
